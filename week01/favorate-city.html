KAMPALA CITY THE, BEST CITY

Kampala is built on seven original hills, including Old Kampala Hill and Kololo Hill, which shaped its early layout and growth. It is one of East Africa’s fastest-growing cities and serves as the political and economic center of Uganda. The city is famous for its vibrant street life, especially the thousands of motorcycle taxis known as boda bodas. Kampala is located near Lake Victoria, the largest tropical lake in the world and a key source of the Nile River. It is also a cultural hub with landmarks like the Uganda National Mosque offering panoramic views of the city.
